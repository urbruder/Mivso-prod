# Team SOP / Playbook Builder Reference

## SOP Anatomy
| Field | What to Capture |
|---|---|
| Purpose | Why this workflow exists and what problem it prevents |
| Trigger | The event that starts the workflow |
| Owner | One role accountable for completion |
| Inputs | Documents, facts, approvals, or client details needed |
| Steps | The ordered actions the owner must complete |
| Handoff | Who receives the output and how they know it is ready |
| Quality Standard | What good looks like |
| Escalation | When the owner must raise the issue to a manager or broker |
| Completion Proof | What shows the step is finished |

## Common Real Estate Team Workflows
- New lead intake and assignment
- Buyer consultation and agency agreement
- Listing appointment prep
- Listing launch and marketing calendar
- Open house preparation and follow-up
- Offer review and counteroffer support
- Contract-to-close handoff
- Inspection and appraisal issue escalation
- Closing week communications
- Post-close review request and nurture
