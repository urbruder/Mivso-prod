# Client Portal Status Mirror Reference

## Client-Safe Status Labels
| Label | Use When |
|---|---|
| Completed | The step is done and no client action is needed |
| In Progress | Work is underway and the team is monitoring it |
| Upcoming | The step is scheduled or expected later |
| Needs Attention | The client or team must act soon |
| Waiting on Third Party | A lender, title company, inspector, HOA, or seller/buyer side is involved |

## Milestone Language Examples
- Offer accepted: Your offer is accepted and the transaction is now moving into the first deadline period.
- Earnest money: Earnest money has been received or is scheduled according to the contract timeline.
- Inspection: Inspection is scheduled or being reviewed.
- Appraisal: Appraisal has been ordered or is waiting for completion.
- Title: Title and closing teams are preparing documents and reviewing requirements.
- Final walkthrough: Final walkthrough is upcoming before closing.
- Closing: Closing is scheduled, subject to final lender, title, and document readiness.
