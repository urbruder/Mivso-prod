# Broker Recruiting Pitch Reference

## Recruit Segments
| Segment | Likely Pain Point | Strong Pitch Angle |
|---|---|---|
| Newer agent | Confusion, lack of structure, low confidence | Training, scripts, accountability, accessible broker support |
| Mid-level producer | Too much admin work and inconsistent marketing | TC support, marketing leverage, systems, transaction consistency |
| Top producer | Plateau, brand constraints, leverage limits | Custom growth plan, operational leverage, listing support, leadership path |
| Small team | Scalability and retention pressure | Shared ops, compliance, recruiting help, team playbook support |
| Returning agent | Rebuilding pipeline | Low-friction reactivation plan, coaching, database strategy |

## Objection Themes
- I do not want to lose my brand.
- I am worried about switching systems.
- My split is better where I am.
- I do not need more training.
- I already have a broker I like.
- I am afraid clients will be confused.

## Proof Point Ideas
Use only facts the broker can support: retention rate, average agent production lift, support response time, marketing turnaround, transaction coordination capacity, lead conversion process, training attendance, or agent testimonial quotes.
