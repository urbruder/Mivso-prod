# Commission Split Calculator Reference

## Commission Order Checklist
Confirm the brokerage policy before applying this order. Common order:
1. Start with sale price and side commission.
2. Calculate gross commission for the side.
3. Apply referral fee if it is paid from gross.
4. Apply split to adjusted gross commission.
5. Apply cap rules to company dollar.
6. Apply brokerage, team, mentor, admin, E&O, and TC fees according to policy.
7. Produce agent net and brokerage net.

## Common Fields
| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Gross commission | Commission before referrals and deductions |
| Adjusted gross commission | Commission after referral or off-top deductions |
| Company dollar | Brokerage share before or after cap, depending on policy |
| Cap remaining | Company dollar still owed before agent reaches cap |
| Agent net | Agent payout after approved deductions |
| Brokerage net | Brokerage retained amount after payouts and fees |

## Review Flags
- Referral fee basis unclear.
- Cap remaining not provided.
- Team split and brokerage split both appear to apply to the same dollars.
- Fees are not tied to written policy.
- DA total does not reconcile to gross commission.
