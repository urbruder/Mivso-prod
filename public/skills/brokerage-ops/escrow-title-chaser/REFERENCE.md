# Escrow & Title Chaser Reference

## Common Missing Items
| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Lender | Loan commitment, closing disclosure, clear-to-close, funding conditions |
| Title | Title commitment, payoff statement, lien release, vesting confirmation |
| Escrow | Settlement statement, escrow instructions, receipt confirmations |
| Client | ID, signatures, funds verification, final document approvals |
| Agent/Broker | Commission instructions, referral instructions, compliance approval |

## Reminder Cadence
- Non-urgent item: follow up every 2-3 business days.
- Deadline within 5 business days: follow up daily until confirmed.
- Closing-critical item within 48 hours: escalate to broker or manager after one missed response window.
- Wire-related item: use secure verification process only and avoid sending sensitive details in casual messages.
