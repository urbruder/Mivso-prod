# Team Transition Communicator Reference

## Client Segment Matrix
| Segment | Main Concern | Message Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Active listing client | Is my listing still being marketed well? | Continuity, marketing plan, new contact, showing and feedback process |
| Active buyer | Will my search or offer be delayed? | Search coverage, showing coordination, offer support, response time |
| Under-contract client | Will deadlines be missed? | Timeline control, TC and broker oversight, next milestone |
| Past client | Why did my agent leave? | Relationship continuity, future support, no unnecessary detail |
| Warm lead | Who should I talk to now? | Simple introduction and low-pressure next step |

## Tone Rules
- Be calm and direct.
- Do not over-explain personnel details.
- Lead with continuity and client care.
- Give one clear next action.
- Confirm the client is not being abandoned.
