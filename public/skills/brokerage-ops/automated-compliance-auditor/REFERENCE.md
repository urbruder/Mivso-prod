# Automated Compliance Auditor Reference

## Issue Types
| Issue Type | Examples | Typical Severity |
|---|---|---|
| Missing document | Required disclosure absent, agency form absent | High or Critical |
| Missing signature | Required party did not sign | High |
| Missing date | Signature date or effective date blank | Medium or High |
| Missing initials | Changed term not acknowledged | Medium or High |
| Inconsistent information | Names, address, price, or dates conflict | High |
| Unreadable document | Scan is blurry or incomplete | Medium |
| Naming or filing issue | Document exists but is misfiled or named poorly | Low |
| Broker judgment | Requirement unclear or transaction-specific | Needs Broker Review |

## Agent Notice Pattern
Use this sequence: friendly opener, transaction identifier, issue, why it matters operationally, exact fix, due date, and who to contact with questions.
